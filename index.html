How SehatGuide Works

From booking to treatment — guided every step. A small fee ensures guide quality and commitment.

1

Book Your Guide

Select your hospital, date, and time. Your request is sent to all on-duty guides at that location.

2

Pay Service Fee

A transparent fee (PKR 500–800) is collected upfront, ensuring committed, professional service.

3

Guide Receives You

Your guide greets you at the entrance and walks you through Reception, OPD, Labs, and Pharmacy.

4
